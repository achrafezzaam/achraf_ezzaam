E-commerce Sales Performance Dashboard

Marketing Campaign Lead List Cleanup

Service Provided: Dataset Cleaning & Health Check

The Challenge:

A marketing team had a lead list of 5,000 contacts with numerous duplicates, inconsistent formatting, and missing information, making it unusable for their new email campaign.

My Solution:

Using Python (Pandas), I de-duplicated the list, standardized country and state names, corrected formatting for phone numbers, and flagged all entries with missing email addresses. I delivered a clean, campaign-ready list.

Tools Used: Python (Pandas), Excel
